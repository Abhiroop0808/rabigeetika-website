Who We Are

Rabigeetika thrives on celebrating the exquisite art of Rabindranath Tagore, embodying his timeless melodies through our performances in New Delhi. Our group is a vibrant collective of passionate artists dedicated to honoring the spirit of Tagore’s creations.

Our journey began with a shared admiration for Tagore's legacy, and it has blossomed into a heartfelt mission to bring his art to life through engaging songs, mesmerizing dances, and powerful theatre. Each performance tells a story, evoking emotion and reverence.

At Rabigeetika, we believe in the power of art to connect people, inspiring a sense of unity and joy within the community.

Songs

Soulful renditions of Tagore's beloved compositions performed with passion and precision.

Dances

Enthralling choreography that breathes life into Tagore's poetic expressions.

Theatre

Engaging theatrical performances inspired by Tagore's profound narratives.
